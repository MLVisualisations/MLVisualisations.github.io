K Nearest Neighbours

K nearest neighbours is the simplest classification algorithm. Given a similarity measure (i.e. distance measure), we classify a new element as the mode of its k nearest neighbours in our training set.

Consider a training set of two dimensional vectors belonging to one of two classes, red or blue. Which class does the green cross belong to?

Possibly the most obvious solution is to look at its nearest neighbour. This requires us to compute the distance between the green cross and all other points in our training set.

Either way, it is quite clear that its nearest neighbour is a blue dot and so we can classify the point as belonging to the blue class. In fact we can classify the whole space using this idea.
